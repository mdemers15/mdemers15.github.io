Your Adams Morgan Record Guides

We Know Our Records.

We buy all kinds of records and record collections, large and small. You're welcome to walk in with small lots, we'll make you a deal on the spot. For medium-to-larger collections it's best to email us. Please include as much info as you can about the collection and photos if you have them. We pay very competitive rates so you get a fair price!

About Us

We don't just sell records. We love to share music and make new friends! In shared space in the heart of Adams Morgan, Tiny Vinyl Shop is a gathering place for neighbors and visitors alike.

You'll find us one block East of Columbia Rd., across from The Line Hotel — right on the D72/D74/C51 bus line.
